About Us

PRAPTH is a passionate team of individuals dedicated to utilizing the power of technology to make a positive impact on society. We are driven by the belief that everyone deserves to be found and reunited with their loved ones, and we are committed to developing innovative solutions to the critical issue of missing person identification.


Our Mission:

Our mission is to leverage the potential of facial recognition technology to create a world where missing individuals are quickly and accurately identified, leading to faster reunions and happier families.

  • Developing and implementing state-of-the-art facial recognition algorithms
  • Collaborating with law enforcement and NGOs
  • Raising awareness and fostering communities
